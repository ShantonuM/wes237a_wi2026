This is a rough diary to make a report out of later.

3/1/26

Laser does not work with the IR receiver in our sensors kit. This is because
the IR receiver is tuned to detect IR at 38 KHz. It has a bandpass filter within
it.
The laser is a contant beam of light and hence, does not work.
Switched to using IR LED. Apparently, a log of commercial laser tag systems use
the same.

3/2/26

First connected +5V pin on joystick module to Arduino Nano ESP32's VBUS - should
have worked, but didn't. The neutral position reading was off, so was the max.

Connected the pin to Nano's 3.3V and it was much better.
